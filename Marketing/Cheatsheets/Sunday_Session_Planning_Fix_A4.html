STEP 1: START WITH LONG-TERM GOALS
  • Ask the Key Question
    "Where do I want these players in 6 months?" Everything flows from here.
  • Technical Goals
    Improved first touch, stronger weak foot, better 1v1 ability, passing accuracy
  • Tactical Goals
    Positioning, decision-making, game understanding, pressing triggers
  • Write Them Down
    Physical targets need to be clear and measurable for tracking progress
STEP 2: CREATE 4-6 WEEK BLOCKS
  • Block Themes
    Block 1: First touch. Block 2: 1v1 attacking. Block 3: Passing under pressure
  • Progressive Difficulty
    Each session builds on the previous, creating compound learning effects
  • Age-Appropriate Focus
    Match complexity to developmental readiness and attention spans
  • Clear Progression
    Skills stick because learning builds systematically week after week

STEP 4: TRACK AND ADAPT
  • Simple Session Notes
    3-4 bullet points after each session. What worked? What needs work?
  • Player Response
    How did they handle new challenges? Energy levels throughout?
  • Skill Assessment
    Which areas need more repetition? What's transferring to matches?

AGES 6-11: FOUNDATION
Focus on ball mastery and confidence building. Keep sessions fun, active, with high success rates. Short explanations, lots of repetition.
AGES 8-12: DEVELOPMENT
Add opposition and decision-making elements. Introduce tactical concepts through games. Balance challenge with enjoyment.
AGES 12+: SPECIALISATION
Introduce tactical complexity and game management. Position-specific work. Higher intensity, match preparation focus.
